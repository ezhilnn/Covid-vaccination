Common Symptoms After COVID-19 Vaccine

After receiving the COVID-19 vaccine, you might experience some common side effects. These usually subside within a few days. The following are common symptoms:

  1. Fatigue: Feeling tired or lethargic.
  2. Headache: Mild to moderate headaches.
  3. Chills or Fever: Experiencing mild fever or chills.
  4. Muscle or Joint Pain: Aching muscles or joints.
  5. Injection Site Reactions: Soreness, redness, or swelling at the injection site.
  6. Nausea: Feeling queasy or experiencing mild gastrointestinal symptoms.
  7. Other Flu-like Symptoms: Such as runny nose, cough, or sore throat.
